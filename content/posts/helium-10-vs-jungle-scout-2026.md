## Product Research Comparison

Both tools excel at finding profitable products. Here's how they compare:

### Helium 10's Black Box

Black Box is Helium 10's flagship product research tool. You can filter by:
- Monthly revenue range
- Review count
- Price range
- Weight and dimensions
- BSR (Best Seller Rank)
- FBA/FBM sellers

The database covers over 450 million products and updates daily.

### Jungle Scout's Product Database

Jungle Scout's approach is slightly different. Their Product Database offers:
- Opportunity Score (unique metric combining demand vs competition)
- Seasonal trends
- Historical data
- Niche Hunter for finding underserved categories

**Verdict:** Both are excellent. Helium 10 offers more filter options, while Jungle Scout's Opportunity Score makes decision-making faster for beginners.

## Keyword Research Comparison

### Helium 10's Cerebro

Cerebro is widely considered the gold standard for Amazon keyword research. Features include:
- Reverse ASIN lookup
- Keyword difficulty scores
- PPC bid estimates
- Search volume trends
- Competitor keyword gaps

### Jungle Scout's Keyword Scout

Keyword Scout is capable but less comprehensive:
- Reverse ASIN lookup
- Search volume data
- PPC suggested bids
- Seasonality trends

**Winner: Helium 10** - Cerebro's depth of data and competitor gap analysis give it a clear edge for serious sellers.
