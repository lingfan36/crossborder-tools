## FAQs

### Which has the best Shopify integration?

All three integrate seamlessly with Shopify. DSers and Spocket are available directly from the Shopify App Store. CJ Dropshipping requires a separate account connection.

### Can I switch platforms later?

Yes, but it requires re-importing products. Start with DSers (free) to validate, then migrate winners to CJ or Spocket.

### Which platform has better customer support?

Spocket offers the best support for English-speaking sellers. CJ Dropshipping support can be slow due to time zones. DSers support is adequate for basic needs.

### Do I need Shopify to use these?

DSers and Spocket require Shopify or WooCommerce. CJ Dropshipping works with most platforms including standalone orders.
