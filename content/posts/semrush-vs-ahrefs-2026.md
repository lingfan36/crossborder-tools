When it comes to SEO tools for e-commerce, [Semrush](/semrush) and [Ahrefs](/ahrefs) are the two heavyweights. Both offer comprehensive features, but they excel in different areas.

In this comparison, we'll break down which tool is better for driving organic traffic to your online store.

## Quick Verdict

| Category | Winner | Why |
|----------|--------|-----|
| **Keyword Research** | Semrush | Larger database, better filters |
| **Backlink Analysis** | Ahrefs | Most comprehensive link index |
| **Content Tools** | Semrush | Built-in SEO writing assistant |
| **Competitor Research** | Tie | Both excellent |
| **E-commerce Features** | Semrush | Product listing ads analysis |
| **Pricing** | Semrush | More features per dollar |

**Bottom Line:** [Semrush](/semrush) is better for e-commerce sellers who need an all-in-one platform. [Ahrefs](/ahrefs) is better for those focused primarily on link building.

- **25+ billion keywords** globally
- **Intent categorization** (transactional, informational, etc.)
- **Keyword difficulty** scores
- **SERP features** analysis
- **Trend data** over time

For e-commerce, the intent filter is invaluableâ€”find keywords where people are ready to buy.

### Ahrefs Keywords Explorer

Ahrefs offers:
- **10+ billion keywords**
- **Click metrics** (searches vs clicks)
- **Parent topic** grouping

**Winner: Semrush** - Larger database and intent classification make it more actionable for product-focused content.

## Backlink Analysis

### Ahrefs Site Explorer

Ahrefs has the industry's best backlink index:
- **35+ trillion** known links
- **Real-time** updates
- **Link velocity** tracking
- **Broken link** opportunities
- **Detailed anchor text** analysis

### Semrush Backlink Analytics

Semrush's backlink tools include:

- **Authority Score** metric
- **Toxic link** detection

**Winner: Ahrefs** - Industry consensus is that Ahrefs' backlink data is more accurate and comprehensive.

## FAQs

### Can I use both Semrush and Ahrefs?

Yes, many SEO professionals use Semrush for content and Ahrefs for backlinks. However, this doubles your costsâ€”only worth it for agencies or large sites.

### Which has better customer support?

Semrush offers more support channels (phone, chat, email) and has a larger knowledge base. Ahrefs relies primarily on email support and their excellent blog.

### Do I really need either for a small store?

If you're just starting, free tools (Google Search Console, Google Analytics) may be enough. Invest in paid tools once you're doing $5k+/month or need to outrank competitors.
