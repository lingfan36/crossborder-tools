## Stage 1: Building Your Store

### Shopify (Required)

[Shopify](/shopify) remains the undisputed king of dropshipping platforms in 2026. Here's why:

- **One-click integrations** with all major dropshipping apps
- **Proven checkout** that maximizes conversions
- **Scalable infrastructure** that handles traffic spikes
- **24/7 support** when things go wrong

**Our Pick:** Start with the Basic plan at $29/mo. Upgrade to Shopify (standard) only after you're doing consistent sales.

### Theme Recommendation

Use a fast, conversion-optimized theme like:
- **Dawn** (free, Shopify's default)
- **Debutify** (freemium, dropshipping-focused)
- [PageFly](/pagefly) for custom landing pages

## Stage 2: Finding Winning Products

This is where most dropshippers fail. Random product selection leads to wasted ad spend.

### Option 1: Minea (Best Overall)

[Minea](/minea) monitors ads across TikTok, Facebook, Pinterest, and Instagram. Features include:

- **Real ad creatives** from competitors
- **Product performance data** over time
- **Supplier links** for quick sourcing
- **Chrome extension** for instant research

**Best for:** Sellers who want comprehensive multi-platform research.

### Option 2: PipiAds (TikTok Focus)

[PipiAds](/pipiads) specializes in TikTok ad intelligence:

- **TikTok-specific** ad database
- **Influencer discovery** for collaborations
- **Trending sounds** and hashtags
- **Direct product links**

**Best for:** Sellers focusing on TikTok Shop and TikTok ads.

### Option 3: Ecomhunt (Budget-Friendly)

[Ecomhunt](/ecomhunt) curates winning products daily:

- **Hand-picked** trending products
- **Supplier links** included
- **Profit calculations** provided
- **Ad targeting** suggestions

**Best for:** Beginners who want curated recommendations.

## Stage 3: Supplier & Fulfillment

### DSers (AliExpress Orders)

[DSers](/dsers) is the official AliExpress dropshipping partner:

✅ **Free plan** available
✅ **Bulk order processing** saves hours
✅ **Supplier optimizer** finds cheaper sources
✅ **Automatic tracking** updates

**Limitations:** Long shipping times (15-45 days).

### CJ Dropshipping (Faster Shipping)

[CJ Dropshipping](/cj-dropshipping) offers warehousing solutions:

✅ **US/EU warehouses** for faster delivery
✅ **Free product sourcing** service
✅ **Custom packaging** options
✅ **No monthly fees**

**Best for:** Scaling stores that need faster shipping.

### Spocket (Premium Suppliers)

[Spocket](/spocket) connects you with US/EU suppliers:

✅ **2-7 day shipping** to Western markets
✅ **Verified, quality** suppliers
✅ **Branded invoicing** included
✅ **Sample orders** available

**Best for:** Premium brands targeting US/EU customers.

## Stage 4: Marketing & Content

### Video Creation with CapCut

[CapCut](/capcut) is the free video editor every dropshipper needs:

- **TikTok templates** for trending formats
- **Auto-captions** in multiple languages
- **Background removal** for product shots
- **Music library** with trending sounds

### AI Videos with HeyGen

[HeyGen](/heygen) creates AI avatar videos:

- **No filming required** — just scripts
- **175+ languages** for global markets
- **UGC-style** videos that convert
- **Product demos** in minutes

### Social Media Automation

[ManyChat](/manychat) automates customer conversations:

- **Instagram DM** automation
- **Facebook Messenger** flows
- **Lead capture** from ads
- **Order status** updates

## Stage 5: Email Marketing & Retention

### Klaviyo (Recommended)

[Klaviyo](/klaviyo) is built for e-commerce:

- **Shopify integration** with one click
- **Abandoned cart** flows built-in
- **Segmentation** by purchase behavior
- **Free up to 250 contacts**

Key flows to set up:
1. Welcome series
2. Abandoned cart recovery
3. Post-purchase follow-up
4. Win-back campaigns

### Mailchimp (Alternative)

[Mailchimp](/mailchimp) offers similar features:

- **User-friendly** interface
- **Free tier** available
- **Landing pages** included
- **Basic automation**

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### 1. Too Many Tools
Start with essentials. Add tools only when you have a specific problem to solve.

### 2. Ignoring Shipping Times
Customers expect fast delivery. Use CJ Dropshipping warehouses or Spocket for faster shipping.

### 3. Skipping Email Marketing
Email generates 25%+ of revenue for successful stores. Set up Klaviyo flows immediately.

### 4. No Video Content
Video ads outperform static images 3:1. Use CapCut and HeyGen from day one.
