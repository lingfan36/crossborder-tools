## Tips for Maximizing Budget Tools

### 1. Use Free Trials Strategically

Both Helium 10 and Jungle Scout offer free trials. Don't start them until you're ready to do serious research. Prepare your product ideas first, then activate the trial.

### 2. Annual Plans Save 20-40%

If you've validated a tool works for you, annual subscriptions offer significant discounts:
- Helium 10 Starter: ~$290/year vs $348
- Jungle Scout Basic: ~$349/year vs $588

### 3. Stack Free + Paid

Use SellerSprite for unlimited product research, and Helium 10 free tier for keyword deep-dives when needed. Best of both worlds.

### 4. Focus on One Marketplace First

Tools often charge per marketplace. Start with one (usually US), validate your business model, then expand.

## FAQs

### Is the Helium 10 free plan enough to start?

For initial product research, yes. But the 20 monthly searches limit means you'll run out quickly during active research phases. Consider upgrading once you've validated your first product.

### Which budget tool has the best Chrome extension?

Helium 10's Xray extension is the most data-rich, even on the Starter plan. SellerSprite's extension is also solid but shows less data.

### Can I run ads without expensive tools?

Yes. Amazon's native Campaign Manager and Search Term Report are free with Seller Central. Third-party PPC tools are nice to have, not need to have.
